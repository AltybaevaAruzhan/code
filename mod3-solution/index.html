About Us

Welcome to The Velvet Fork, your cozy corner in the heart of Almaty. We are a unique café that combines the warmth of a coffee shop with the charm of a library. Our mission is to provide a relaxing environment where you can enjoy delicious food, great coffee, and meet new people. Whether you're here to study, work, or just unwind, The Velvet Fork is the perfect place for you.
